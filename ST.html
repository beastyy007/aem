SIMULATION TRAININGS

Simulation training means creating realistic, immersive scenarios that replicate real-world situations, allowing individuals to practice skills and decision-making in a safe, risk-free environment. It's a form of experiential learning that helps trainees develop practical skills, refine their knowledge, and build confidence before applying them in real-life situations.
