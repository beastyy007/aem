EMERGENCY & ICU CARE TRAINING (EICT)

EICT is a study about Basics of lots of topics which are going to be Helpful in Day to Day Practise in Any Department of Hospital like Emergency, ICU, Wards, OT or Clinics.

TOPICS :

  • ECG - Module 1 / Module 2
  • Patient Assessment
  • Chest X-Ray / Basic CT Scan
  • Basic Life Support / CPR - Hands on Training
  • Chest Pain / Breathlessness / Stroke
  • Trauma Protocols + Skills / Fracture - Hands on Training
  • Intubation - Hands on Training
  • Suturing - Hands on Training
  • AED / Defibrillator - Hands on Training
  • IV / Catheterization - Hands on Practice
  • IM / RT Insertion - Hands on Practice
  • OBGY Training – Hands on Practice
  • Ortho Skills – Slab Application - Hands on Practice
  • Advanced Cardiac Support - Algorhythms
  • Simulation Based Training – Real Case Base Scenarios – Practice

WHO CAN ATTEND :

  • • Medical Officers
  • • MBBS
  • • BHMS / BAMS
  • • Interns
  • • ICU
  • • ER Staff

  • Duration : 6 Sundays
  • Certified By : Academy of Emergency Medicine
  • Timing : 8:30 AM To 3:00 PM (IST)
